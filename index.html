About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Simpatico HR Consultancy is one of the leading online recruitment HR consultancies located in Perintalmanna, Kerala, providing various HR services across the world. We help employers find matching candidates for their existing vacancies, offering local talent from specific countries where our clients need candidates. We do not provide visa sponsorship and focus solely on serving citizens within the respective countries.

Current status: Open for collaboration and recruitment services

Our Services

  • Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO): We offer end-to-end recruitment services for businesses, from candidate sourcing to onboarding.
  • Job Portal Development: We build job portals for clients to manage their job listings and candidate applications effectively.
  • Candidate Sourcing: We specialize in sourcing candidates for specific regions and industries, ensuring the right fit for your organization.
  • Employer Branding: We help companies develop a strong employer brand to attract the right talent in a competitive market.
  • HR Consulting: Our HR experts offer consultation on various HR processes, employee management, and retention strategies.

How It Works

  • Step 1 — Consultation: We begin by understanding your hiring requirements and organizational culture to tailor our approach.
  • Step 2 — Candidate Sourcing: Our team sources candidates from relevant regions and industries based on your specific needs.
  • Step 3 — Screening and Shortlisting: We conduct thorough screening and shortlisting of candidates to ensure the best fit for your organization.
  • Step 4 — Interview and Selection: We coordinate interviews and assist with the final selection process to ensure a seamless experience.
  • Step 5 — Onboarding: We help manage the onboarding process, ensuring smooth integration of new hires into your company.

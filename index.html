About Simpatico HR Consultancy

Simpatico HR Consultancy is one of the leading online recruitment HR consultancies located in Perintalmanna, Kerala, providing various HR services across the world. Our approach ensures that the right talent meets the right opportunity, through strategic sourcing and efficient recruitment processes.

Our Services

  • Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO): Comprehensive recruitment services that help businesses streamline their hiring processes.
  • Job Portal Development: Custom job listing platforms to connect candidates with opportunities.
  • Candidate Sourcing: Specialized sourcing by region, industry, and skillset to find the best talent.
  • Employer Branding: Helping companies build a strong reputation as an employer of choice.
  • HR Consulting: Providing HR strategies and solutions that promote employee engagement and retention.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Consultation – We discuss your recruitment needs.
  • Step 2: Candidate Sourcing – We source the best talent for you.
  • Step 3: Screening & Shortlisting – We evaluate and shortlist candidates based on your criteria.
  • Step 4: Interview & Selection – We conduct interviews to ensure the best match.
  • Step 5: Onboarding – We assist with smooth onboarding for the selected candidate.
